I'm listening to Genesis and learning about the nation of Israel's relationship with other family names. Egyptians, Ammonites, Moabites. He was loosly related to these people.(see aside) I have a big family. It more than doubled when I was married. I'm about to be divorced. But not from the experiences that influenced my divorce. It's made me want to create a timeline of every single family name that has become associated with the Woodall name. As it stands now, I have lost all these names but that's for various reasons. Timline of how Woodall's became intwined with the Couture's who have history with the Shippy's, Vosies, and Riggs.
