Talking, Listening, or not.

At the most basic level, wouldn't you agree that most people have a mouth and two ears. Those mouths don't listen. They only talk. Conversely, ears don't talk. They only listen. So by that rationale, those with ears and mouths fall into one of four options...

talk with their mouths and listen with their ears.
talk with their mouths but don't listen with their ears.
listen with their ears but don't talk with their mouths
don't listen with their ears or talk with their mouths

They don't talk about anything of substance. Of course, they use their mouths but the words, knowledge, and understanding is not worth listening to.
