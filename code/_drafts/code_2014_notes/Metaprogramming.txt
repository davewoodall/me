By calling Object#method( ), you get the method itself as a Method object, which you can later execute with Method#call( ). A Method object is sim- ilar to a lambda, with an important difference: A lambda is evaluated in the scope it’s defined in (it’s a closure, remember?), while a Method is evaluated in the scope of its object. - pg 125??
